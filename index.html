About K218b

Discovered in 2024, K218b represents one of the most fascinating exoplanets ever found, with characteristics that challenge our understanding of planetary formation.

Physical Properties

  • Diameter: 142,984 km (1.12x Jupiter)
  • Mass: 2.3x Jupiter
  • Gravity: 3.2x Earth
  • Composition: Gas giant with solid core

Orbital Characteristics

  • Star: K-type orange dwarf
  • Distance from star: 0.8 AU
  • Orbital period: 218 Earth days
  • Rotational period: 16 Earth hours

Atmosphere

  • Composition: Hydrogen, Helium, Methane
  • Pressure: 12x Earth atmosphere
  • Temperature range: -120°C to 80°C
  • Unique feature: Bioluminescent clouds

Unique Features

K218b possesses several extraordinary characteristics that make it one of the most fascinating exoplanets in our galactic neighborhood.
